About 646 Area Code

Area code 646 serves Manhattan exclusively and was introduced in 1999 as an overlay to the prestigious 212 area code. It primarily serves businesses, newer residents, and mobile services in Manhattan's most affluent neighborhoods and business districts.

Key Facts:

  • Introduced: July 1, 1999
  • Type: Overlay area code (overlays 212)
  • Primary Use: Manhattan businesses, residents, mobile phones
  • Population Served: 1.6+ million Manhattan residents & workers
Manhattan Area Codes
  • 212 (Original - 1947)
  • 646 (Overlay - 1999)
  • 917 (Mobile - citywide)
  • 929 (Newer - citywide)
  • 332 (Latest - citywide)
Note: 646 is Manhattan-only, unlike citywide codes
